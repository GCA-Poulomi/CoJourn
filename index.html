🌎 CoJourn for Climate Action

An Interactive Program Outline

Program Overview

Stay connected and build momentum from the Climate Justice Summit by registering for CoJourn for Climate Action.

Learn more: cojourn.org/for-climate-action

Time Commitment

🕒 30 minutes/week to check in with your accountability partner.

Participant Benefits

  • 🌱 Discern your area of focus and impact
  • 📚 Continue your climate education
  • 🏡 Align lifestyle changes with your values
  • 📢 Increase your activism
  • 💚 Sustain and deepen your work
  • ✅ Actually follow through on your intentions

Program Components

📣 Group Launch

A kickoff event at the Climate Summit + access to a resource portal on the CoJourn framework.

🤝 Accountability Partner

Get matched with a partner interested in climate action—or bring your own.

📑 Resource List

Education, action, and community-care tools—tailored for all experience levels.

📆 Monthly Group Meetings (Zoom)

Optional online gatherings to check-in, share, and stay inspired.

📬 Weekly Support Emails

Tips, reminders, and suggested action goals delivered straight to your inbox.

🌐 This is an interactive program outline. For full program details and registration, visit cojourn.org.
